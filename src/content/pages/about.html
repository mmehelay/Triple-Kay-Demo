About Company Title

At Triple Kay Demolition, we don’t just tear things down — we make space for new beginnings. With over 5 years of collective expertise across Toronto and the GTA, we’ve built a reputation for providing reliable and efficient demolition services. From complete interior demolitions to exterior dismantling and post-renovation clean-ups, we handle every project with precision and care.

We pride ourselves on being dependable, ensuring every project is completed on time and within budget. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart. Whether it’s a home renovation or a large commercial site, we guarantee a clean, safe, and ready-to-build space for the next chapter.

At Triple Kay Demolition, we believe every project is a new beginning. We’re dedicated to doing the job right, ensuring our clients are always ready for their next steps.

Kingsley Appiah CEO-Founder
